About This Site

This website is about the fairly modern internet phenomenon known as the "Rickroll", where an unsuspecting victim is directed to the music video of the 1987 song "Never Gonna Give You Up" by Rick Astley. The means of deception can vary, but the most common technique is linking the music video, using a QR code or concealing the music video within a seemingly innocent video.

This site aims to educate the oblivious Internet user about the Rickroll, its history, how to avoid it and even how to Rickroll others through the use of a clearly structured website with an easy to use navigation bar.
